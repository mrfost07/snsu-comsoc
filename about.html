About Us.

Learn more about SNSU Computer Society — who we are, what drives us, and the community we're building for tomorrow's tech leaders.

Who we are

Passionate about technology.

SNSU Computer Society is the premier technology-focused student organization at Surigao del Norte State University. Founded to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world tech, we've grown into a thriving community of innovators.

We provide opportunities to develop technical skills, engage with industry professionals, and build projects that create real impact — whether you're writing your first line of code or shipping production software.

See what we do

01 — Mission

What we do.

To cultivate a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts through hands-on learning experiences, meaningful collaboration, and the empowerment of students with real-world skills and knowledge to thrive in the digital age.

02 — Vision

Where we're going.

To be the leading student technology organization in the region, recognized for producing innovative leaders who drive technological advancement and create positive, lasting change in the community and beyond.

History

Our journey.

The milestones that shaped the Computer Society into what it is today.

2018

Organization founded

A small group of passionate CS students formed the Computer Society with a shared vision.

2019

First hackathon

Inaugural hackathon — 30 participants, 8 project submissions, one unforgettable weekend.

2021

100 members

Reached 100 active members despite the challenges of remote and hybrid learning.

2023

First Tech Summit

Hosted our first large-scale summit with industry speakers and regional participation.

2026

Present day

200+ members, 50+ events hosted, building the next generation of technology leaders.

Activities

What we do.

01 — Learn

Workshops & Seminars

Regular hands-on sessions covering web dev, mobile apps, AI/ML, cybersecurity, and emerging tech — led by members and industry pros.

02 — Build

Hackathons & Comp

Competitive events that challenge members to ship real solutions, sharpen problem-solving skills, and compete for recognition.

03 — Connect

Community & Network

Social events, peer mentorship, and industry connections that help members build professional networks and lasting friendships.
