About

Building the future, one line at a time.

01 — Mission

What we do.

To cultivate a culture of innovation and lifelong learning in programming, machine learning, and data science. To build a collaborative and inclusive community where students strengthen their technical expertise, achieve professional growth, and apply computer science knowledge to address real-world challenges. To enrich members' academic journey through dynamic activities, purposeful initiatives, and effective information-sharing, making their experience in the Computer Society both meaningful and memorable.

02 — Vision

Where we're going.

To be one of the university's leading organizations that empower BSCS students to become innovative and skilled ICT practitioners, upholding technological excellence, professional integrity, and contributing meaningfully to the sustainable development of Caraga and beyond.
